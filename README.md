# Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a retail dataset and building interactive dashboards to uncover key business insights.

The goal is to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and regional distribution, and convert these insights into actionable business recommendations.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze sales, profit, and quantity trends  
- Identify top-performing products and categories  
- Understand customer behavior and contribution  
- Evaluate regional performance  
- Build interactive dashboards for decision-making  

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)  
- Matplotlib and Seaborn (Visualization)  
- Tableau (Dashboard)  
- Jupyter Notebook  

---

## Dataset
- The dataset used in this project is sourced from a YouTube site-(Data by Brian) .  
- It includes multiple tables:
  - Orders  
  - Customers  
  - Products  
  - Location

- Customer Distribution and Top Customers  
- Interactive Filters (Region, Category, Year)  

---

## Key Insights
- Technology category drives most sales but has lower profit margins  
- A small number of customers and products contribute majority of revenue  
- Customer engagement is low (most customers place few orders)  
- West region performs best, while Central region underperforms  
- High sales do not always lead to high profit  

---

## Business Recommendations
- Improve pricing strategy to increase profit margins  
- Reduce excessive discounting  
- Focus on high-margin products and categories  
- Retain high-value customers and increase engagement  
- Optimize performance in low-performing regions
